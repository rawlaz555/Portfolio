I am a freelance graphic designer who started working remotely with my self built set up for about 6 years. Being a fast learner, I started work using my most favored software such as the Adobe suite, HTML, CSS and Javascript to create art that brings life to many websites and social media platforms. Mainly Adobe Photoshop, After effects, Adobe Illustrator, Figma, InDesign. The projects i am most proud of revolve around designing Menus for restaurants, banners for promotion, Emoticons for chat rooms, Animated backgrounds for pending screens, GIFs for Alerts, Logo's for companies and social media influencers. I have also taken some self projects that involve figma designs, and some Web design. My greatest inspiration comes from Art, for me it is like a global language, words spoken with pleasing imagery and branding, fortunately today we have digital art that can help bring my ideas to light. Additionally, i love photography for a hobby and digital painting. I am very well spoken in English and know conversational level Greek and Arabic.
